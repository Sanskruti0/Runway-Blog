GCDS Spring 2023

Favorite Trend : Crochet

Crochet, the age-old craft, made a triumphant return to the runways in 2023, weaving its way into the fabric of the fashion world. Designers embraced the artistry of crochet, showcasing intricate patterns and delicate textures. Giuliano Calza, the designer and co-founder of GCDS, set a tone of seriousness as he initiated the discussion on the new collection. Calza's collections skillfully merge his pop sensibilities with GenZ nostalgia, presenting an array of vibrant, playful, and alluring looks.

Cherry Red : A Winter Valentine

The fashion scene turned cherry red into the life of the party! This bold and playful hue stole the spotlight on runways, transforming everything from chic couture to street-savvy styles. De Sarno's collection naturally offered a parade of towering platform shoes, mini lace-trim slip dresses, and lots of cherry (actually called Rosso Ancora, a hue which has deep ties with Gucci's roots).Cherry red became the 'it' color,making 2023 the year of cherry-red chicness!

Gucci RTW 2024

Givenchy Spring 2023

Denim Reimagined

Denim underwent a captivating reimagination that stormed the runways with a fresh and innovative spirit. Designers embraced a reinvented denim narrative, pushing boundaries with unconventional cuts, embellishments, and avant-garde styling. In the Spring 2023 collection by Givenchy, Bella Hadid rocked a denim look that was pure magic! A fusion of chic and edgy vibes, with denim that danced on the edge of bold and playful.

Special Statement : Spray on dress

In the fashion spectacle of Coperni's Spring/Summer 2023 collection, the spotlight was undeniably on the iconic 'Spray-On Dress.' This revolutionary piece redefined runway moments, capturing attention as one of the most iconic fashion statements ever. It wasn't just a runway; it was a wild ride through the most iconic fashion moment ever. Parisian fashion brand Coperni used Fabrican's sprayable, liquid fibre to spray a dress onto a model. This dress wasn't just fashion; it was a party, and everyone was invited!

Coperni Spring/Summer 2023

Cargo Chic : Unmatched Comfort

In the whimsical world of fashion, guess who's strutting back into the spotlight? It's none other than cargo pants, making a triumphant return to the style scene! For Spring/Summer 2024, Seoul-based fashion label Andersson Bell looks to fuse contrasting cultural style codes to form its own quintessential uniform. It's a cargo craze, and we're here for the fun and functionality of this nostalgic yet contemporary wardrobe revival!

Ferragamo SS23

Reviving '90s Nostalgia

In a surprising turn of style, the '90s are reclaiming the runway spotlight in 2023. Runways are becoming a vibrant showcase of retro-chic, with a modern twist on classic '90s silhouettes. Experimentations with wooden beads, bonded linens and cottons, and sculpted leather also helped make the Ferragamo collection feel distinctive. As fashion enthusiasts eagerly embrace the resurgence of these iconic trends, it's clear that the spirit of the '90s is alive and thriving in the 21st century.

Blooms of Elegance : Rosette

Blossoming trend on the fashion runways, rosette dresses are stealing the show in 2023. These enchanting and feminine creations, adorned with delicate floral embellishments, are making a powerful statement in the world of haute couture. The runways are transforming into a garden of glamour, with the rosette dress trend proving that timeless beauty never goes out of style. Fashionistas are embracing the allure of these floral-inspired ensembles, turning every catwalk into a celebration of nature's grace and fashion finesse.
